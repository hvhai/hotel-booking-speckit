Hotel Booking Application – Terms of Service

1. Membership & Eligibility
   - Only registered members (Classic, Gold, Diamond) may book rooms.
   - Membership level is assigned at booking and cannot be changed retroactively for existing bookings.

2. Discounts
   - Classic: 0% discount.
   - Gold: 10% discount.
   - Diamond: 20% discount.
   - Discounts are automatically applied to booking totals and reflected in invoices.

3. Booking & Cancellations
   - Bookings can only be made and managed by logged-in members.
   - Only future bookings (with a check-in date in the future) are eligible for cancellation.
   - Members may only view and cancel their own bookings.

4. Refund & Penalty Policy
   - Cancellations more than 48 hours before check-in: 100% refund.
   - Cancellations 24–48 hours before check-in: 50% refund.
   - Cancellations less than 24 hours before check-in: no refund.
   - Refunds are processed automatically via the payment gateway.

5. Transparency
   - The system provides clear information on discounts, refund, and penalty amounts before and after booking or cancellation.
   - All calculations are accurate and auditable.

6. Administration
   - Only administrators may create users or change membership levels.
   - Administrators may view all bookings; regular users may only view their own.

7. General
   - All times are calculated in the hotel’s local timezone.
   - Use of the system implies acceptance of these terms.
   - The hotel reserves the right to update these terms as needed.
